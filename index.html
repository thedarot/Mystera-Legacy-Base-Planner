Coded by GreyHope / Updated by Darot
What is it?
A base planner for Mystera Legacy that automatically calculates the cost in materials for your constructions.

How to use?
1. Select an item on the left
2. Left click to place
3. Right click to remove
4. Shift + click to fill lines/rectangles
5. Shift + Alt + click to erase pattern

Saving your project?
• Name your project in the text field
• Use "💾 Save" to save as editable .json
• Use "📂 Load" to load previous project
• Use "Export" to generate image or text

Enjoy the planner! 🎮
